Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Roulette Picker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

Roulette Picker does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2.2 Participant Names

The participant names you enter in the app are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the participant list or uninstalling the app

2.3 Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information including:

  • Device identifiers (such as IDFA for iOS)
  • IP address
  • App usage data
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

The locally stored participant names are used solely to:

  • Display your saved participants when you open the app
  • Enable the roulette selection feature
  • Provide a seamless user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

All participant data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanism (SharedPreferences). We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

5.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to provide personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Allow Apps to Request to Track (disable)
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (disable)

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain content specifically targeted at children.

7. Data Retention

Participant names are stored on your device until you:

  • Delete them manually using the "Delete All" feature
  • Remove individual participants
  • Uninstall the application

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data (visible in the app)
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no international data transfer involved in using this app.
